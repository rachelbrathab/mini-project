Chrome Extension Setup

Install the Serenra Focus extension from your downloaded ZIP file.

  1. Click Download Extension in the sidebar to download smart-study-focus-extension.zip.
  2. Extract the ZIP file on your computer.
  3. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions.
  4. Turn on Developer mode (top-right corner).
  5. Click Load unpacked.
  6. Select the extracted focus-extension folder.
  7. Pin the extension and keep your planner open while studying.

Troubleshooting

Extension not updating? Click Reload on the extension card in chrome://extensions.

Focus rules not syncing? Keep the planner tab open and refresh your Progress page.

Download failed? Log in again and retry from the sidebar download link.
